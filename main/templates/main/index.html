FeynmanIt

This application was created with the intention of implementing the infamous learning technique called “The Feynman Technique”. As a way to provide others a chance to truly learn and understand topics from the comfort of their electronical devices.

Features

Folders

For better organization, this application comes with the functionality of folders. You can add, move, delete notes from certain folders with ease.

Notes

This feature is the heart and soul of this application. Allowing you to have a template for future learnings. With accessible additions of links, to custom iterations to provide greater clarity.

Progress

View your monthly progress displayed on a heatmap. Designed specifically for this application.

How to use this application

Step 01:

Write what you know

The first iteration of step one should be information you know about a topic prior to researching. This way you can build the foundation of what you should research. From then on, each iteration would be the result updated knowledge after researching.

Step 01a:

Links

This step was added by the developers to assist users in providing sources to what helped them further understand gaps in their iterations. An example of how to use links:

  1. Write knowledge on topic in step 1
  2. Identify gaps in knowledge in step 2
  3. Research topic to fill gaps
  4. Provide research links in step 1a
  5. Write updated knowledge on topic in step 1
  6. Repeat until you can confidently and simply describe topic in step 3

Step 02:

Gaps in explanation

This is the step where you will attempt to identify any gaps in your present expanation of the topic. This can be done by describing in very simple terms the topic to someone else, and or as done in the computer world by talking to a toy duck. If you are still unsure about certain aspects in the explanation, this is where you would write those uncertanties to see your thought process and solidify your understanding of the topic.
